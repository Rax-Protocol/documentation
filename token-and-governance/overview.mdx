The $RAX token is deployed on Base at address `0x3051a5e7AB247eBF2baa7Af9e3B574b344B175E3`.

It serves as the access and coordination mechanism for the RAX Protocol ecosystem. Its primary purpose is to align usage and participation with long-term protocol health, rather than to serve as a speculative asset.

---

### Primary Functions

**Feature Access** — $RAX holdings determine the user's access tier. Holding tokens unlocks progressively advanced platform features, from basic analytics to autonomous vault management.

**Protocol Alignment** — Token-gated access ensures that users with meaningful participation in the ecosystem have access to the most powerful tools.

---

### Access Tiers

| Tier | Minimum $RAX | Features Unlocked |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| Free | 0 | Risk Overview, basic exposure data |
| Holder | 1 | Full vault rankings, advanced filters, alerts & monitoring |
| Pro | 1,000 | Advanced simulations, allocation engine presets, full exposure analysis |
| Power | 10,000 | Decision log export, full settings, governance preview, RaxBot access |

---

### What the Token Is Not

$RAX does not represent equity, dividends, or guaranteed returns. Its value is derived from functional utility and participation within the protocol, not from financial guarantees.
